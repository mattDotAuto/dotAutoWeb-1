About Us

Welcome to Dot Automation Pty Ltd, a leading Microsoft Business Central and Power Platform consulting firm based in Northern New South Wales. We specialize in functional and semi-technical consultation, empowering businesses to unlock their full potential by leveraging the power of Microsoft's innovative tools. Our team of experts is dedicated to helping organisations streamline their operations, enhance productivity, and drive digital transformation.

By harnessing the capabilities of Microsoft Business Central and Power Platform, businesses can revolutionize their processes and decision-making. Business Central offers comprehensive enterprise resource planning (ERP) functionality, enabling organisations to manage finances, operations, sales, and customer service more effectively. Power Platform, comprising Power BI, Power Apps, and Power Automate, facilitates rapid application development, data visualisation, and workflow automation. Together, these tools deliver an integrated ecosystem that supports agility, growth, and efficiency.

At Dot Automation Pty Ltd, we believe in a personalised approach, tailoring our services to meet the unique needs of each client. Through in-depth analysis and strategic implementation, we ensure that our clients reap the benefits of Microsoft's cutting-edge technology, optimizing their business processes and staying ahead in today's competitive landscape.
